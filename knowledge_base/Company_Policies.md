# Company Policies Knowledge Base

## HR Policies
- **Leave Policy**: Annual 20 paid days; apply 7 days prior via HR portal. Sick leave unlimited with doctor's note.
- **Remote Work**: Up to 3 days/week; requires manager approval and stable internet.
- **Dress Code**: Business casual Mon-Thu; casual Fridays.

## IT Policies
- **Password Rules**: 12+ chars, change quarterly; no reuse of last 5.
- **Data Security**: Encrypt sensitive files; report breaches within 1 hour.

## Code of Conduct
- Harassment zero-tolerance; report to HR anonymously.
- Gifts from vendors under $50 only.
