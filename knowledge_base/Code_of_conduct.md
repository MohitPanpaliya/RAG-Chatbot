### What constitutes harassment?
Any unwelcome conduct based on gender, race, religion, or other protected characteristics, including verbal, physical, or digital behavior. Report anonymously to HR.

### Can I accept gifts from clients?
Gifts under $50 are permitted; declare higher-value items. No cash gifts allowed.

### What are the rules on office relationships?
Romantic relationships between reporting lines are prohibited; disclose to HR to avoid conflicts.
