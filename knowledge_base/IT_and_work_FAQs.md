### What are password requirements?
Minimum 12 characters, including uppercase, numbers, symbols. Change quarterly; no reuse of last 5 passwords.

### Is remote work allowed?
Hybrid model: 3 days remote max per week with manager approval. Full remote requires special circumstances.

### What happens during disciplinary action?
Progressive steps: verbal warning, written warning, performance plan, termination. Appeals go to HR within 5 days.
